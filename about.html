Our Mission

Providing care and nourishment to street dogs through innovative technology

The Problem

Street dogs often face hunger, malnutrition, and a lack of regular care. Many compassionate individuals want to help but may not be able to maintain a consistent feeding schedule.

Our Solution

The Automated Dog Feeder Project aims to provide a sustainable solution by creating smart feeders that can be placed in areas with street dog populations. These feeders:

  • Dispense food at regular intervals
  • Monitor the dogs using ESP32 CAM technology
  • Operate autonomously on power banks with minimal maintenance
  • Allow remote monitoring and control via WiFi

Project Goals

We aim to:

  • Improve the nutritional health of street dogs
  • Monitor population health trends
  • Create a scalable, low-cost solution that can be implemented widely
  • Build a community of supporters who can maintain and expand the network
